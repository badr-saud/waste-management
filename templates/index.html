Toxic Gas Classifier

Binary Support Vector Machine (SVM) classifier to detect toxic gas based on sensor measurements.

Features:

  • Fill Level
  • Weight
  • Gas Concentration

Classes:

  • Normal: Normal reading
  • Full: Container is full
